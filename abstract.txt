Music composition has traditionally required years of formal training, making it largely inaccessible to most aspiring creators. This paper presents SonicAI, an AI-powered interactive music education and generation platform built on a teaching-first philosophy — guiding users through music theory concepts including chords, scales, rhythm, notes, and instrumentation before generating a final audio track.
Unlike black-box music generators, SonicAI follows a structured plan → learn → customize → generate workflow, transforming passive generation into an active learning experience. The platform integrates Meta AI's MusicGen (facebook/musicgen-small) — a single-stage autoregressive Transformer over a 32kHz EnCodec tokenizer — with optional vocal synthesis via Bark (Suno AI) and a DSP fallback engine for GPU-constrained environments. The backend is powered by FastAPI, Celery + Redis, and PostgreSQL, while the frontend uses React 19 + Vite, Zustand, and WaveSurfer.js. Post-processing is handled via pydub and scipy, with full Docker containerization
